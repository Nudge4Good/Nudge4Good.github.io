The Challenge & Opportunity

Challenge

Consumers struggle with making healthy and sustainable food choices amidst overwhelming options and misinformation.

Opportunity

Leverage AI to personalize recommendations, nudge behaviors, and explain decisions for better outcomes.

What is Nudge4Good?

Nudge4Good is a research and innovation project developing AI-driven solutions for personalized food recommendations. We combine advanced machine learning with behavioral science to help users make healthier, more sustainable food choices while providing transparency through explainable AI.
