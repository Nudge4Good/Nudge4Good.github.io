Our Technology

Advanced AI solutions for personalized food recommendations and behavioral change.

Technology Overview

Nudge4Good leverages cutting-edge AI technologies to create personalized, explainable, and effective food recommendation systems that nudge users towards healthier and more sustainable choices.

AI Personalization

Advanced recommender systems using machine learning algorithms to analyze user preferences, health data, and contextual information for highly personalized food suggestions.

Behavioral Nudging

Psychologically-informed techniques to gently influence user behavior, encouraging healthier choices without restricting freedom.

Explainable AI (XAI)

Transparent AI models that provide clear explanations for recommendations, building user trust and enabling better decision-making.

LLM Integration

Large Language Models for conversational interfaces and natural language processing to enhance user interaction and understanding.
